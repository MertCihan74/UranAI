Introduction

Welcome to Coffee Fortune Telling. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We collect the following information during registration:

  • Name and surname

The information we collect is used to:

We use OpenAI's GPT API to generate your fortune readings. When you use our service:

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

Data Storage and Security

  • Your personal information is stored securely
  • We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your data

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
